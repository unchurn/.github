# Unchurn

[Português Brasil](./README-PT.md) | [Español](./README-ES.md)

**Unchurn** is a SaaS platform built to help subscription-based businesses reduce churn and increase retention through proactive communication, smart automation, and customer lifecycle data.

> We turn churn signals into retention actions.

---

## What we do

Unchurn centralizes key customer journey events and turns them into practical retention workflows.

With Unchurn, companies can:

- Identify risk moments before cancellation
- Connect payment, email, and messaging platforms
- Automate personalized communications at the right time
- Understand why users leave and how to recover them
- Create workflows for retention, renewal, recovery, and feedback

---

## Main use cases

- Upcoming renewal reminders
- Failed payment recovery
- Trial ending campaigns
- Support actions before cancellation
- Cancellation feedback collection
- Customer health monitoring
- Churn risk identification

---

## Platform capabilities

Unchurn is being built as a modern, secure, and scalable SaaS foundation.

### Product

- Retention automation
- Email and WhatsApp communication
- Workflows based on customer events
- Integrations with payment platforms
- Cancellation feedback collection and analysis
- Event monitoring for trial ending, failed payment, renewal, and cancellation

### Platform

- Multi-tenant model with organizations
- Members, roles, and team permissions
- Secure OAuth authentication with Google and GitHub
- Persistent sessions with Redis
- Rate limiting
- API keys with a complete lifecycle
- Access scopes and permissions
- Foundation ready for webhooks, integrations, and event ingestion
- Audit logs for sensitive actions

## Our vision

Retention should not be reactive.

Unchurn helps product, support, and growth teams act **before** churn happens, using the right message, in the right channel, at the right time.

We want to make retention simpler, smarter, and more accessible for companies that depend on recurring revenue.
